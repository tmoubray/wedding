Our Story

Emily and Todd met as Todd was hammering out a Green Day song on the community Grand piano, located in an art gallery frequented by Emily. She had recently started studying there as a graduate student. As Todd was finishing up the outro to Layla she noticed his poorly stitched knockoff Joey Votto jersey and the slightly tilted Pokey Reese like hat he wore on his head. He had a pequiliar beard and a sort of half grin she found endearing. She could hardly resist.
She asked him what else he could play. He played her a medly of Jackson Browne, an improvised version of lean on me, and the theme to good times. She was sold. They went on a date that night. Although life took a few turns they never forgot each other, and eventually ended up standing on the Scioto river at a quarter after seven just as the sun hit the river in June 2016. They were engaged.
