Our Story

Todd and Emily met in an art gallery on the campus of Wright State University where Todd was playing the piano and Emily was studying for the evening. They went on a date that night. Although life would take them different places in the years to come they stayed in contact and reunited some time later while Emily was living in Columbus. Upon moving to Columbus as well to persue a new career and Emily, Todd proposed, and they are engaged to be married in August of 2017.
